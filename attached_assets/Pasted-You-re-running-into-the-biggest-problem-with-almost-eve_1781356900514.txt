You're running into the biggest problem with almost every "AI SaaS template" built on Replit, Bolt, Lovable, or Cursor:

**It looks like an AI product, but underneath it's mostly hardcoded demo data.**

Looking at your screenshots:

* Lead Finder → fake business names generated from templates
* Analyzer → fixed scores and recommendations
* Business Intelligence → canned summaries
* Outreach → template responses
* Proposal → placeholder content
* Website Mockup → static templates
* Marketing Tools → pre-written outputs

That's not really AI. It's UI pretending to be AI.

and removing all hardcoded scores, recommendations, business names, and templates. That's what will make it feel like ChatGPT instead of a demo.
