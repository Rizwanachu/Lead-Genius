What you're describing is essentially an **AI Lead Generation + Outreach Agency System**.

The goal is:

**Input:**

* Country = USA
* Niche = Cafe

**Output:**

* List of cafes without websites
* Contact details collected
* Personalized outreach sent automatically
* Follow-ups handled
* Meetings booked
* Potential clients converted

This is possible, but there are some important limitations:

* Email sending is possible.
* Social media outreach is partially possible.
* WhatsApp automation is heavily restricted unless you use the official WhatsApp Business API.
* Fully automatic client conversion is unrealistic. The AI can do 80-90% of the work, but closing deals usually requires human involvement.

---

# Architecture

## Agent 1: Lead Finder

User enters:

```text
Country: USA
Business Type: Cafe
```

Agent searches:

* Google Maps
* Yelp
* Facebook Pages
* Instagram Business Profiles

Extract:

```text
Business Name
Location
Phone
Email
Website
Instagram
Facebook
```

---

## Agent 2: Website Detection

Checks:

```text
Does business have website?
```

If:

```text
website = null
```

or

```text
facebook only
```

Move to next stage.

Example:

```text
Joe's Cafe
Instagram Only
No Website
```

Potential lead.

---

## Agent 3: Lead Scoring

Score every lead.

Example:

| Factor             | Points |
| ------------------ | ------ |
| No website         | +40    |
| Has Instagram      | +20    |
| Active business    | +20    |
| Good reviews       | +10    |
| Multiple locations | +10    |

Score:

```text
0-100
```

Only contact:

```text
Score > 70
```

---

## Agent 4: AI Website Audit

AI analyzes:

```text
Instagram
Facebook
Google Maps
```

Generates:

```text
Business summary
Problems
Website opportunity
```

Example:

```text
Cafe has 400 reviews.
No online ordering.
No website.
No booking page.

Potential website value:
High.
```

---

## Agent 5: Personalized Outreach Writer

Instead of:

```text
Hi sir need website?
```

Generate:

```text
Hi Sarah,

I came across your cafe in Austin and noticed you have great reviews but don't currently have a website.

Many customers search directly on Google before visiting.

I made a few suggestions that could help increase online orders and visibility...
```

This should be AI-generated per business.

Use:

* OpenAI
* Claude
* Gemini

---

## Agent 6: Multi-Channel Outreach

### Email

Send through:

* Resend
* SendGrid
* Mailgun

Track:

```text
Opened?
Clicked?
Replied?
```

---

### Instagram

Not recommended to automate aggressively.

Instead:

* AI drafts message
* Human approves
* Send manually

Instagram automation can get accounts restricted.

---

### Facebook

Similar approach.

AI drafts.
Human sends.

---

### WhatsApp

Use official:

[WhatsApp Business Platform](https://business.whatsapp.com/products/platform?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

Avoid unofficial bots.

A stronger business model than "website agency" would be:

> Find businesses without websites → generate a website automatically with AI → include a live preview link in the first outreach email.

That makes your offer much harder to ignore because you're showing the solution before they buy. For local cafes, salons, restaurants, gyms, dentists, and contractors, this approach often gets significantly better response rates than simply asking whether they need a website.
