You're getting close to an actual agency operating system. At this point, I'd stop adding random features and focus on the few features that create the biggest jump in client acquisition.

### What's still missing

## 1. Real Lead Discovery Engine (Highest Priority)

Right now it sounds like you have the sales and CRM side.

What actually makes money is:

```text
Country
State
City
Business Type
↓
Find Businesses
↓
Analyze
↓
Outreach
```

Add:

* Google Maps search
* Google Places API integration
* CSV import
* Bulk lead enrichment
* Duplicate detection
* Website detection

Without this, the system is mostly operating on manually added leads.

---

## 2. AI Opportunity Scanner

When opening a lead, add:

```text
Top 10 problems found
```

Examples:

* No website
* Website not mobile-friendly
* Missing Google reviews strategy
* No Instagram posting
* Slow website
* Missing call-to-action
* No online booking

Then rank:

```text
Easy Win
Medium Win
High Value Win
```

This gives you instant talking points.

---

## 3. Sales Battle Card

For every lead generate:

```text
Best Hook
Best Offer
Likely Objection
Recommended Price
Close Probability
```

Example:

```text
Best Hook:
You have 450 reviews but no online booking.

Likely Objection:
Already busy enough.

Response:
Focus on reducing missed calls and automating bookings.
```

This is gold during outreach.

---

## 4. AI Call Preparation Page

Before a meeting:

Generate:

* Business summary
* Owner profile
* Competitor weaknesses
* Questions to ask
* Recommended offer
* Pricing recommendation

This makes you sound prepared even if you've never spoken to them before.

---

## 6. Client Fulfillment Workspace

Once they become a client:

Track:

```text
Client Onboarding
Content Creation
Website Build
SEO
Ads
Reporting
```

Most agency systems stop at closing.

The real value is managing delivery.

## 7. AI Website Generator

This is the feature I'd build next.

Workflow:

```text
Lead Found
↓
No Website
↓
Generate Demo Site
↓
Generate Share Link
↓
Include Link In Outreach
```

Imagine sending:

> "I built a sample homepage for your business. Would you like to see it?"

That's much stronger than:

> "Do you need a website?"

## Version 3 Roadmap

If I were building the next version, I'd prioritize:

1. Real Google Maps Lead Discovery
2. AI Website Generator
3. Sales Battle Cards
4. Call Preparation Dashboard
5. Service Recommendation Engine
6. Dynamic Proposal Pricing
7. Agency Revenue Dashboard

Those features move it from a nice CRM into a system that can actually help you acquire and close website, SEO, and social media clients consistently.
